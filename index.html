About me

I think it is important for individuals to understand the importance of creativity and its relevance to all fields of work and study. My goal is to combine my creativity with my technical knowledge to produce unique works of art that are expressive of who I am.

The combination of the arts and sciences has become something that I wish to explore further and I hope my explorations can inspire new and exciting designs and illustrations.

Through passion and the techiniques I have learned, creating interesting digital paintings has become by far my favorite artistic/technical skill I have developed. However, with my enthusiasm for all things art, design, and color it its hard to put a finger on what my favorite medium to work in is. With a constantly growing skillset I am excited to take on any project that comes my way with a smile.
